About Us

Our Vision

To be the leading partner in sustainable mining town development, delivering essential commodities while fostering socio-economic growth and community prosperity.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing reliable supply and delivery of commodities, while investing in community development initiatives that enhance the quality of life, economic opportunities, and environmental sustainability in South Africa.

We are commited to the Community!

Community Empowerment

MTT Trive is dedicated to sustainable community development through initiatives such as scholarship programs, vocational training, healthcare services, infrastructure development, entrepreneurship support, and environmental conservation projects.

These efforts aim to provide financial assistance, skill training, improve healthcare, enhance infrastructure, support local businesses, and promote environmental protection and restoration.

Collaborating for Impact

Healthcare Providers

Driving Growth

MTT Trive is dedicated to fostering growth through job creation, entrepreneurship, and skills development.

The company generates employment opportunities within its supply chain and operations while supporting small and medium enterprises (SMEs) through subcontracting and mentorship programs.

MTT Trive also focuses on skills development by offering training workshops and certification programs in collaboration with educational institutions and industry experts
